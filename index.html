Frequently Asked Questions

What is Todogether?

Todogether is a task management tool designed for group collaboration, allowing teams to assign, confirm, and track tasks efficiently.

How does task confirmation work?

When you're assigned a task, you must "Accept" it to confirm that you've seen it. If you don't, the system will remind you.

What happens if I don't accept a task?

If you don't confirm within the set time, Todogether will send reminders via notifications, and if necessary, trigger an automated call.

Does Todogether integrate with my calendar?

Yes! Todogether analyzes your schedule and suggests the best time to complete tasks based on difficulty and availability.
